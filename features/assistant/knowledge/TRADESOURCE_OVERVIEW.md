# TradeSource Overview

## What TradeSource Is
TradeSource is a contractor-to-contractor network for vetted painters and contractors.

It helps contractors who have too much work connect with vetted contractors who are looking for work — through a structured, trust-based workflow.

It is painter-first. Painting and renovation overflow work are the primary job types.

## Core Value
TradeSource solves the contractor overflow problem:
- contractors who are overloaded can distribute work to trusted network partners
- contractors who need work can find vetted opportunities within the network
- vetting and approval create trust between both sides

## How It Works
A contractor posts an overflow job to the network.
Other vetted contractors browse and express interest.
The posting contractor awards the job.
Work moves through the platform with messaging, tracking, and trust signals.

## Who Uses It
All users in this MVP are contractors.

Contractor-to-contractor only. No homeowner or client participation in the current version.

## Painter-First Positioning
TradeSource starts with painting contractors as its primary user base and primary job type.
The assistant's strongest expertise is painting scope, rough pricing, and rough materials guidance.

## Problems TradeSource Solves
For contractors with too much work:
- unreliable subcontractors
- no structured way to distribute overflow
- trust gaps when outsourcing

For contractors looking for work:
- low-quality leads
- unclear job scope
- wasted time on bad fits

For both:
- unclear next steps
- weak visibility into job progress
- vetting and trust gaps

## What TradeSource Is Not
- Not a homeowner marketplace
- Not a client-to-contractor matching platform
- Not a public lead dump
- Not a legal, tax, or permit authority
- Not a guarantee of price, timing, or workmanship

## Platform Feel
Practical, professional, trustworthy, simple to navigate, trade-aware.

## MVP Status
This version is contractor-to-contractor only.
No homeowners, clients, or property managers as active participants.
Homeowner features are not active and are not yet implemented.
