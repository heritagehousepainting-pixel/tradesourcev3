## 1. Review System

After every completed job, the poster marks the job as "complete." At that point, both parties have the opportunity to leave:

- A 1–5 star review
- A written comment

**FOUNDER FACT** — This is the confirmed review mechanism.

**Important:** Reviews are a major part of TradeSource. TradeSource is building a network through trust.

---

## 2. Review Display Behavior

Reviews display Google-style:

- The user has an **overall visible score**
- Next to that score is a **clickable review count**
- When a user clicks the review count, they can see **all reviews**

**FOUNDER FACT** — This is the confirmed display pattern.

The visual structure is:
- Overall star score (e.g., "4.8")
- Review count next to it (e.g., "12 reviews")
- Clicking the count reveals individual reviews with written comments

This applies to reviews visible on a user's profile/dashboard page.

---

## 3. Reputation Visibility

Reviews help build a contractor's score and reputation inside TradeSource.

This reputation should help users win more jobs — whether they are the poster or the subcontractor.

**FOUNDER FACT** — Reputation and review information should be visible on the platform.

**FOUNDER FACT** — Reviews should be visible on a user's dashboard/profile page so other approved users can see them.

Reputation visibility is confirmed as a platform feature. The specific mechanics of how reputation scores are calculated (e.g., weighted average, recency weighting) are not yet documented — those are product details requiring separate specification.

---

## 4. Suspension and Removal Authority

TradeSource can suspend or remove users from the network.

**Confirmed grounds for suspension or removal:**

- Fraud
- Fake or incorrect documents
- No-shows
- Poor workmanship
- Scams
- Repeated complaints
- Hate speech
- Inappropriate language in messaging

**FOUNDER FACT** — Anything dealing with trust or reputation should be visible on the platform where appropriate.

**Not yet confirmed:** Appeal process, escalation mechanism, or formal reinstatement policy. Do not invent these in content.

---

## 5. Dispute Boundaries

**FOUNDER FACT — TradeSource does not resolve disputes between connected users.**

Once two parties connect through the network, TradeSource does not act as the decision-maker on who is right.

**FOUNDER FACT** — TradeSource may suspend or remove users based on behavior, documentation issues, complaints, trust violations, or platform safety concerns.

**FOUNDER FACT** — TradeSource is a trust gatekeeper for the network, not a mediator or arbitrator of job disputes.

This means:
- Do not position TradeSource as handling disputes between contractors
- Do not promise resolution services
- Do not claim TradeSource will adjudicate who is at fault in a job disagreement
- Trust and safety concerns may result in removal, but this is separate from dispute resolution
