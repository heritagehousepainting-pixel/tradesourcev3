## The Joining Process

### Step 1: Application

A painter applies through the TradeSource app.

### Step 2: Required Information

The applicant must provide:
- Basic information
- Contractor license information
- Experience (type, duration, scope)
- One external link (e.g., Google Business Profile, social media, website, or review site)
- Proof of insurance
- W-9

### Step 3: Review

After submission, TradeSource reviews the application and verifies that all files and information are legitimate.

**Review criteria:**
- All required information is present
- Information is accurate and not fraudulent
- Documents can be verified

**Denial conditions:**
- Required information is missing
- Information is incorrect or fraudulent
- Documents cannot be verified

### Step 4: Approval Email

If approved, the applicant receives an email.

### Step 5: Account Activation

Through the email link, the approved user:
1. Creates a password
2. Gains full access to TradeSource

---

## Access Levels

### Unapproved / Pending Users

- Can see a preview of the open jobs page (a limited, non-functional view)
- Cannot see actual job details
- Cannot access full platform features
- Do not have full account access

### Approved Users

- Full access to TradeSource
- Can see all open jobs
- Can post jobs
- Can respond to jobs
- Account is active upon password creation
