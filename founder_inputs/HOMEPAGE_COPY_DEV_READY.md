## SECTION 3 — The Problem

**Heading:**
The way contractors find subs right now is broken.

**Body:**
When a job needs help and your regular crew can't cover it, the options are the same as they've always been:

Facebook Marketplace.
Craigslist.
A Google search.
Business cards from the paint store.
A referral from someone who knows someone.

You don't know who you're calling. You don't know if they show up. You don't know if they do good work. And if you pick wrong, you lose the job, lose the customer, or both.

No contractor should have to trust their business to a random Google search.

## SECTION 4 — What TradeSource Is

**Heading:**
A vetted network. Fixed rates. No guessing.

**Body:**
TradeSource is a private contractor network built around one mechanism: you post a job at your rate, vetted contractors in the network see it and respond, and you choose who you send it to.

No bidding. No one undercuts your price to get the job.
No lead fees. You're not paying for a list of maybe-interested contractors.
No wondering if the person who shows up actually does the work.

Every contractor in the network is verified before they get access — license, insurance, W-9, experience, and at least one real customer review.

## SECTION 6 — How It Works

**Heading:**
How TradeSource works.

**Step 1 — Apply**
Submit your basic business information, contractor license, proof of insurance, W-9, experience, and at least one link to your work.

**Step 2 — We review your application**
Every application is reviewed personally. We verify your documents and check that everything is legitimate before you get access.

**Step 3 — Get approved and access the network**
Once approved, you receive an email and create your password. You get full access to the network of vetted painting contractors.

**Step 4 — Post overflow work at your rate**
When you have work that needs a sub, post it at a fixed price. Describe the scope, set the timeline, and post. Contractors in the network see it and respond.

**Step 5 — Choose who you want to work with**
Review profiles, see experience and past work. You decide who gets the job — no algorithm, no bidding, no surprises.

## SECTION 7 — How We Vet

**Heading:**
How we vet every contractor in the network.

**Subheading:**
Every contractor is verified before they get access. Here's exactly what we check:

**Vetting item 1 — Valid PA Contractor License**
We verify your license number against Pennsylvania state records before you get access.

**Vetting item 2 — Proof of Insurance**
We require a current certificate of insurance. No insurance, no exceptions.

**Vetting item 3 — W-9 Tax Documentation**
We collect a W-9 to confirm your business identity and tax information.

**Vetting item 4 — Trade Experience Verification**
We verify that you have documented experience in your trade — not just a license, but actual work history.

**Vetting item 5 — At Least One External Review**
We confirm at least one review from a real customer or trade reference — a Google Business Profile, Houzz, Angi, or equivalent.
